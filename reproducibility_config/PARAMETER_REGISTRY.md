## Explicitly out of scope

- Plant-grade TMY, plant SCADA, receiver tube-temperature measurements, and calibrated absolute heat-flux safety limits are not included.
- The AFD-style flux proxy is baseline-relative and numerical; it should not be cited as allowable flux-density compliance.
- The fast annual proxy annualizes default-aiming optical tables over public weather only; it should not be cited as bankable annual yield, dispatch validation, or full annual custom-aimpoint performance.
- The multi-year annual-proxy gate uses public NASA POWER weather years only; it should not be cited as measured plant operation.
- The annual-proxy interpolation gate tests numerical mapping choices for the default-aiming optical tables only; it should not be cited as measured plant operation or annual custom aiming.
- The flux-resolution gate is a SolarPILOT default-aiming discretization check only; it should not be cited as receiver thermal certification.
- The flux-day sampling gate is a SolarPILOT default-aiming sampling-density check only; it should not be cited as annual custom aiming, cross-code validation, or receiver thermal certification.
- The 20 unavailable heliostat records are not reconstructed; the missing-20 audit is a geometry-sensitivity stress test only.
- SolarPILOT custom aimpoints and terrain-relative z are not passed through the current PySAM bridge.
- Reduced PySolTrace rows are sampled representative-condition checks, not full-field annual certification.
- The SolarPILOT default-aiming bridge and PySolTrace direct gate are cross-engine evidence, but not a same-custom-aimpoint cross-code round robin.
- The cost-normalized score is a screening indicator only and is not an LCOE or retrofit-economic result.
